Who are we?

We're Resolve Information Technology and we're based in Malvern, Worcestershire. We are specialists in providing a comprehensive range of IT and support services for small and medium sized businesses.

Our aim is to be a single point of contact for your IT services and products, delivering these at a sensible price in a friendly, yet professional, manner.

We know from experience that every client wants a fast response, competitive pricing and flexibility in the solutions offered. This is what we do!





As a small business, we understand that both time and money are important - downtime can cost both. We use high quality equipment and choose our solutions partners carefully to ensure that you get the best user experience possible. Our support personnel and engineers are honest, qualified and friendly and will provide you with a fast and reliable service.

Whether you're starting out in business and are looking for the tools to get you going or you're an established business with an existing infrastructure, we can advise, improve, and support. Our expertise covers consultancy; network design and implementation; server specification, installation and maintenance; security and remote working solutions; telecoms provision and systems installation; PC supply, installation and support cover - basically, if it's IT related, we can help you!
